How to Play

  • Goal: Capture all enemy cities to win.
  • Armies capture cities by moving onto them.
  • Select: Tap a unit to select it (highlighted).
  • Move: Tap a destination tile to move the selected unit.
  • Production: Tap a city you own to set what it builds.
  • Turns: Every 30s auto-advances, or click End Turn early.
  • Combat: Move onto an enemy unit — attacker wins 60% of the time.
  • Fog of War: You only see tiles near your units/cities.
  • Fuel: Fighters (20 moves) and Bombers (10 moves) crash if they run out!
